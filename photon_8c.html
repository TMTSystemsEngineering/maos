Compute subaperture star flux from magnitude, subaperture size, sample period, etc.

Parameters:
[in]zbSky background and zero magnitude flux
[out]Npnumber of total signal at each wvl.
[out]Nptottotal signal (sum of Np).
[out]Nbtotnumber of background photon per pixel
[out]QCSNRsignal to noise ratio for a Quadcell
[out]QCNEAnoise equivalent angle for a Quadcell with Nyquist sampling
[in]nwvlnumber of wavelength.
[in]wvlsvector of wavelength
[in]magsvector of magnitudes
[in]dxsasubaperture side length
[in]iscirclewhere the subaperture is circle/part of a circle. true for TT/TTFA sensors.
[in]pixthetapixel extense in radian
[in]dtsampling period in seconds
[in]zazenith angle
[in]strehlStrehl of the image. set to 1 for full flux.
[in]imperrnmImplementation error in nm
[in]thruputend to end optical throughput
[in]qedetector quantum efficiency.
[in]rnedetector read out noise.
