Returns the transpose of a average gradient operator that converts the OPDs defined on xloc to subapertures defines on saloc with ploc as the intermediate pupil plane.

Parameters:
xlocthe grid on which OPDs are defined
plocthe grid on the aperture plan
ampthe amplitude on ploc
salocCoordinate of the subapertures
saorc0: saloc is the center of the subapertures. 1: saloc is the origin of the subapertures.
scalecone effect
displacedisplacement due to beam angle (2 vector). similar as accphi routines
do_partial1: use points that are outside of by close to a subaperture. 0: do not use.
