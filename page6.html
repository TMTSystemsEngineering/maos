Profiling MAOS

MAOS is a pthread enabled software, and in the current stage, the gprof based profiling does not generate useful information.

Instead, we have to reply on system wide profilers, such as the oprofile.

Prepare:

opcontrol --callgraph=16

opcontrol --start

Then Run MAOS

View profile:

opcontrol --stop

opcontrol --dump

opreport -cgf -l maos | gprof2dot.py -f oprofile | dot -Tpng -o output.png

or

opgprof maos | gprof2dot.py | dot -Tpng -o output.png

gprof2dot.py can be found in the scripts folder.
