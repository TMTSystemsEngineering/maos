range variation

The sodium range variation is specified through the LLT configuration key llt.fnrange in llt_CL.song or llt_SL.conf. When supplied by user, use powfs0_llt.fnrange. The file should contain additional change (wrt fnprof) of sodium height in meters with dimension nx1 or nxnLGS where n can be less than, equal to or greater than the length of simulation and nLGS is the number of LGS in this powfs (6 for NFIRAOS).
