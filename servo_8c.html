Optimize the type II servo gains by balancing errors due to noise and signal propagation.

Written: 2010-06-11

Tested OK: 2010-06-11

2011-01-17: The optimization process is quite slow, but the result is only dependent on the sigman and fs. psdin does not change during the simulation. Built a lookup table in skyc using various sigman and interpolate to get ress, resn, and gain.

2012-03-28: Cleaned up this routine. groupped similar calculations together. Change g2=a from g=sqrt(a) Limit maximum gain to 0.5.

sigman is a dmat array of all wanted sigman. Returns a cellarray of a dmat of [g0, a, T, res_n, res_sig]
