Closed loop simulation main loop.

It calls init_simu() to initialize the simulation struct. Then calls genscreen() to generate atmospheric turbulence screens. Then for every time step, it calls perfevl() to evaluate performance, wfsgrad() to do wfs measurement, reconstruct() to do tomography and DM fit, filter() to do DM command filtering. In MOAO mode, it call calls moao_recon() for MOAO DM fitting.
