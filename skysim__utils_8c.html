convert mod vector to ngs WFS cloc and add to it's opd or complex pupil function.

Input/Output: wvf (complex pupil function). Input: wvl: wavelength. modm: the mode vector cloc: the subaperture coarse sampled loc. fpc: focus piston coupling. (mean(x^2+y^2) to eliminate piston term in focus) thetax, thetay: direction of WFS. parms: parms

Parameters:
[in,out]wvfcomplex pupil function
[in]wvlthe wavelength
[in]modmthe NGS mode vector
[in]clocthe subaperture coarse sampled loc
[in]fpcfocus piston coupling. (mean(x^2+y^2) to eliminate piston term in focus)
[in]thetaxdirection of WFS
[in]thetaydirection of WFS
[in]parmsthe parms
