Detailed Description

Wavefront reconstruction and DM fitting routines.

Since these are related to reconstruction, we don't have access to dmreal, which is the actual location of DM actuators, and only available in simulation. dmint should be used for dm actuator commands.

Function Documentation

void tomofit ( SIM_T *  simu)

Calls tomo() and fit() to do the tomography and DM fit. Do error signal and split tomography. In closedloop mode, the gradients are from time step isim-1.

void reconstruct ( SIM_T *  simu)

Wavefront reconstruction. call tomofit() to do tomo()/fit() or lsr() to do least square reconstruction.
